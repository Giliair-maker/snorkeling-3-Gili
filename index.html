Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your snorkeling adventure

Do I need to know how to swim to snorkel?

While basic swimming skills are recommended, we provide life jackets for all participants and have guides who can assist non-swimmers. Our shallow snorkeling spots are perfect for beginners, and our experienced guides will ensure your safety throughout the experience.

What should I bring for the snorkeling trip?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a towel, a change of clothes, and a waterproof camera if you have one. We provide all snorkeling equipment including masks, snorkels, and fins. Don't forget your sense of adventure!

Is snorkeling safe for children?

Yes! We have special equipment for children and family-friendly snorkeling spots. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult, and we offer discounted rates for kids. Our guides are trained to work with families and ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience.

What is the best time of year for snorkeling in Gili Islands?

The Gili Islands offer excellent snorkeling conditions year-round. However, the dry season from April to October typically provides the best visibility and calmest waters. During the wet season, visibility can still be good, and there are often fewer tourists.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled tour for a full refund. For cancellations within 24 hours, we offer a 50% refund or the option to reschedule. In case of bad weather, we will either reschedule your tour or provide a full refund.
